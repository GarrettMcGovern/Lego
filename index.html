About my Collection

A friend of mine owned Lego’s and in the past I had some Lego sets. I always found the Lego Star Wars sets to be interesting as I am a huge Star Wars nerd. I eventually decided to get a couple of Lego Star Wars sets in September 2022 that I thought were cool and from then, I’ve bought a ton of sets making my collection around 20 sets. Most of my sets are from the original trilogy of Star Wars since that is my favorite era.
